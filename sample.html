The carrier's FCR of 78% appeared within target. However, 22% of interactions generated a repeat contact within 7 days. Transfer chains averaged 3.2 handoffs per resolution. Escalation bounce rate was 8.4% — meaning nearly 1 in 12 escalated tickets returned to the original tier without resolution.

The diagnostic mapped these patterns to four structural fault lines. The largest — Field Revisit Normalization — accounted for $1.2M in annual leakage that was invisible to the BI team because repeat contacts were logged as new interactions, not continuations of the same resolution chain.

None of this appeared in any of the carrier's 14 existing dashboards. The CPR gap of $38.80 per resolution was structural, not seasonal, and had been compounding for over two years.
